BFLUID IV
This formulation of water, amino acids, electrolytes, vitamin B1, and carbohydrates is administered through the peripheral vein for patients with mild hypoproteinemia or mild malnutrition due to inadequate oral intake, and before and after surgery.

HINEX JELLY
Hinex Jelly Corn Flavor 300g pouch is a high protein low fat with fiber semi-solid nutrient which is a novel enteral nutrition for easy oral intake of patients requiring oral nutritional supplement.

Procateral Hydrochloride MEPTIN
A bronchdilator, Procaterol HCI (Meptin) is indicated for relief of dyspnea and other symptoms caused by respiratory obstructive disturbance in bronchial asthma, acute and chonic bronchitis, and pulmonary emphysema.

REBAMIPIDE MUCOSTA
A cytoprotective agent, Rebamipide Mucosta acts against gastric mucosal lesions (erosions, bleeding, redness and edema) in diseases such as gastric ulcer, acute gastritis, and acute exacerbation of chronic gastritis. It has also been shown to be effective in prevention of NSAID-induced gastropathy.

AMINOLEBAN ORAL
An orange flavored dietary supplement, Amino Acids Aminoleban Oral contains high concentration of branched-chain amino acids, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins and minerals used to improve the nutritional state of patients with liver impairment including those with hepatic encephalopathy and patients with protein energy malnutrition.

NEO-MUNE Neo-mune Vanilla and Melon Flavor 400g can is a food for special medical purposes and a formula powder with glutamine, arganine and fish oil for the dietary management of critically ill patients who need high protein and high caloric dient management.
